About

Research first. Then design. Then something that runs.

I'm a UX designer and researcher with 2+ years across SaaS, B2B, B2C, and e-commerce products. My process starts with users, not screens — interviews and usability sessions shape what gets designed, and testing decides what survives.

What separates my work: I don't stop at the Figma handoff. I prototype in code-adjacent tools and ship working artifacts, which means my designs get pressure-tested against reality instead of staying theoretical. And I report findings the way they happened — when three of six screens failed testing, that's what the case study says.
